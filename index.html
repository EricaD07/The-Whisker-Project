Pet Care Tips for New Owners

First time adopting? We've got you covered with essential tips to help your new cat thrive.

Nutrition Basics

Feed high-quality cat food appropriate for your cat's age. Provide fresh water daily and avoid giving human food.

Regular Vet Visits

Schedule annual check-ups and keep vaccinations current. We'll provide all medical records from our care.

Litter Box Care

Keep the litter box clean by scooping daily. Place it in a quiet, accessible location away from food and water.

Love & Enrichment

Spend quality time playing and bonding. Provide scratching posts, toys, and cozy spots for napping.

Need More Information?

Every cat has unique needs, and we're here to help. Learn more about daily care, feeding, grooming, and creating a comfortable home for your new companion.
